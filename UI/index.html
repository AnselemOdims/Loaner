Welcome to Loaner,

We are a consumer finance company and an online lending platform that provide short term loans to individuals. This will help solve the problem of financial inclusion as a way to alleviate poverty and empower low income earners. We are committed to providing consumer credit to our target market in the most efficient and convenient manner while also ensuring best practices.

Our Mission

We are committed to providing consumer credit and loans to our target market, which is low income earners, in the most efficient and convenient manner. Our vision is to be the top online loan entity in the whole of Africa in particular and the World in general. Providing customer-centric financial solutions for all strata of the market, through user-friendly technological innovations.

The Best Rates

Our services are completely free for most home and investment loans.
